SUBMIT BOX TOPS

Even though Box Tops is going mobile, you may still receive clipped Box Tops at school for some time while older packages remain in stores. Just follow these simple, step-by-step instructions to submit those Box Tops and have them count toward your school's earnings check.

Fall submission deadline: Nov 1
Spring submission deadline: Mar 1
GO TO SUBMISSION FORM
ALREADY SUBMITTED? CHECK YOUR SUBMISSION STATUS

HOW TO SUBMIT

YOUR SCHOOL’S CLIPPED BOX TOPS

1

SORT BOX TOPS INTO THREE PILES

BOX TOPS CLIPS

Bundle into groups of 50 using rubber bands, envelopes or baggies. Label each bundle with "50" on the outside.

COMPLETED COLLECTION SHEETS

Staple together to total 50 Box Tops per packet.

BONUS BOX TOPSCERTIFICATES

Bundle all these special certificates together, keeping them separate from regular clipped Box Tops and collection sheets.

Tips

  • All these bundles can be submitted in one package.
  • Box Tops clips don't need to be perfectly trimmed, but the expiration date and product code should be clearly visible on each one.
  • Remove any Box Tops clips or bonus certificates that have expired.
  • If you have any retailer eBoxTops™ codes, do not mail these with your submission. These 14-digit codes must be redeemed online in order to receive credit for your school. Redeem a code now.
2

FILL OUT THE ONLINE SUBMISSION FORM

You must use the current submission form. This tells us how many Box Tops you are submitting for your school. You must have a unique form for every package you send. Packages without submission forms cannot be processed.

3

PREPARE AND MAIL YOUR PACKAGE

Staple your bundle of Bonus Box Tops certificates to your submission form. Then, put everything into your package in this order:

BOX TOPS CLIPS at the bottom
COMPLETED COLLECTION SHEETS next
SUBMISSION FORM &BONUS CERTIFICATES on the top

Every package must have a unique submission form showing the number of Box Tops that are in that particular package.

SEND YOUR BOX TOPS TO:

  • Box Tops for Education
  • 350 Victor St.
  • Highland Park, MI 48203

Tips

  • You can submit packages anytime throughout the year; however, they need to be postmarked on or before the deadline to be included in the next batch of school checks.
  • Check the postmark rules of the mail carrier you choose to use. If your package is received after 6 PM, some carriers will postmark your package as the next day.
  • We recommend saving your postal receipt and tracking your shipment.
