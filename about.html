AMERICA’S SCHOOLS LOVE BOX TOPS!

For over 20 years, Box Tops for Education has given families an easy way to earn cash for their school, with products they already buy.


Now you can simply scan your store receipt with the Box Tops mobile app to identify participating products and instantly add cash to your school's earnings online.

$868,070,032

Total school earnings with Box Tops since 1996...and counting.

Our Story

1996

Box Tops clips appear on select Big G cereals, such as Cheerios™, Total™ and Lucky Charms™. Families can clip Box Tops and send them to school, where they are submitted and redeemed for cash.

2004

The Box Tops for Education™ program doubles to include brands such as Pillsbury™, Old El Paso™ and Green Giant. Over 82,000 participating schools have earned more than $100 million with Box Tops.

2010

Schools across the nation have earned over $300 million with Box Tops.
